Roles

Husband

Male: 30-32 years

Lead role. Should be able to show friendly nature, anger, and empathy.

Wife

Female: 25-27 years

Lead role. Should have jovial nature and comfortable to show pain/cry naturally. Must be able to do their own minimal makeup.

Kid

Girl: 5-6 years

Minimal speaking role. Must be able to take direction and behave naturally. On location parents are responsible for kids safety

Husband's Friend

Male: 30-33 years

Main support to the lead role. Should look stressed out and should have a week old beard on the day of shooting

Husband's Father

Male: 50-60 years

Fair skinned, american accent english speaking. Actor will have to speak few Hindi words as per script
