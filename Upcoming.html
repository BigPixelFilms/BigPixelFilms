Actors and Roles

We are looking for actors to play roles in the movie. Prior experience helps, but not required... passion over takes everything. Must be able to drive to shooting location, and be available during on weekends of March. Please dress appropriately for the role. There is no compensation, but will credt actors in video and copyright to upload the video on thier social platform. Snacks may be provided on the location.

Husband

Male: 30-32 years

Lead role. Should be able to show friendly nature, anger, and empathy.

Wife

Female: 25-27 years

Lead role. Should have jovial nature and comfortable to to pain/cry naturally. Must be able to do their own makeup.

Kid

Girl: 5-6 years

Minimal speaking role. Must be able to take direction and behave naturally. On location parents are responsible for kids safety

Husband's Friend

Male: 30-33 years

Main support to the lead role. Should look stressed out and should have a week old beard on the day of shooting

Husband's Father

Male: 50-60 years

Should have deep voice. Calm face and spiritual personality is added advantage. Hair (or beard) must not be black colored, grey of white hair should be visible on the day of shooting
